Introduction

Butterfly Cycle (“the App”, “we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

Butterfly Cycle does not collect, store, or share any personal information on our servers.

All data you enter in the App (including period dates, symptoms, moods, or any other health-related notes) is stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to this information.

2. Data Use

Since we do not collect data, we do not use your information for analytics, advertising, or any other purpose.

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party analytics, advertising frameworks, or external SDKs that collect user data.

4. Data Security

Your data never leaves your device and remains under your control. If you delete the App, all locally stored data will be removed from your device.

5. Children’s Privacy

Butterfly Cycle is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any material changes will be reflected in the updated version of this document and the effective date above.
